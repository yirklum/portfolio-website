Website Development

The heart of our business is creating customized, one-of-a kind websites for our clients. Our job is to take your ideas and transform them into something solid – a web presence that meets your goals and reflects your distinct personality. From purchasing a domain to launching a finished website, Full Stack Media will guide you through the entire process. Our services include:

  • Website Design:Do you have a detailed plan for each page of your website? We'll build it, pixel by pixel. Do you have a vague sense of what you want to convey, but aren't sure what that looks like? We'll help you develop your ideas. No idea what you want? We'll provide options. Our designers are well versed in UX (user experience) and UI (user interface) principles. They integrate form and function to create websites that are visually appealing and easy to navigate. We will work one-on-one with you to develop a final product you can be proud of.
  • Domain Registration and Website Hosting:Registering a domain name, choosing a hosting service and deploying a website can be very confusing for the uninitiated. Full Stack Media can take care of all of these tasks for you, allowing you to focus on more important things. We will work with you, assessing your needs and budget requirements, to find the right hosting solution for your website.
  • Responsive Layout:With the advent of smartphones and tablets, it is critical that a website or app look just as good on a small screen as it does on a laptop or desktop computer. All of our websites are designed to be completely responsive, meaning they will render on a wide range of screen sizes. Whether your viewers are using a smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer, they will always see the best possible version of your website.
  • SEO:The best website in the world is useless if no one sees it. One of the most critical aspects of promoting your website is utilizing SEO (search engine optimization) best practices. Our SEO experts implement a broad range of strategies to boost a website’s Google search rankings.
  • Content Creation: Too busy running your business to write a lot of text? Looking for a more polished tone? Do you lack the time or equipment required to create the high quality photos or graphics necessary for a professional website? We provide content for those who need additional copy and graphics to fill out their pages. Whether you need help with website copy, blog posts, or even press releases, we can assist you. Writing for the web is a very particular skill – one that our experienced copywriters have mastered. In addition, our design professionals can provide a range of visuals, from carefully curated stock photos to custom infographics. Visit our content writing and graphic design pages to learn more.
  • Front and Back End Development:Our full stack developers write custom code for your website, building each page from the ground up. Rather than using common templates utilized by services such as Wordpress – which make your website look like a thousand others – Full Stack Media can tailor every detail to your unique needs. We handle both the front end of your site (what the user sees) and the back end (what makes the site function), giving you complete control.
