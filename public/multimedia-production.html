Multimedia Production

While a simple website with well-written text and basic graphics is enough to meet the needs of many businesses, some clients desire a little something more. In a world of ubiquitous YouTube videos and attention grabbing podcasts, having multimedia elements can give a business a huge boost. Full Stack Media has the tools and the talent to assist you in creating, editing and distributing a variety of media. Our services include:

  • Photography:Nothing says “amateur website” like low-quality photographs. Snapping a few pictures on a phone – even adding a filter or two –may be sufficient for a Facebook post. However, high quality photos are an absolute necessity for any professional website. Our talented photographers will use state-of-the art cameras, lights and editing software to create the beautiful images that will represent you to the world. Whether you need headshots for a biography, candid shots of an event, or photos of your products, Full Stack Media can meet your needs. Furthermore, if you already have a photo – but it is not quite right – our editors can alter it to your satisfaction.
  • Video Production:While photos and graphics are essential visual tools, some things can only be conveyed through video. From instructional videos to funny clips of animals, some things you just have to see play out. Adding video to a your website – or to other platforms, such as YouTube – is a great way to attract attention and hold interest. A charming welcome message or an illuminating product demonstration can connect to a customer in a way that text alone cannot. Full Stack Media has the tools and experience to produce high quality video content, letting you show your best side to your audience.
  • Podcasts:The number of podcasts being produced has skyrocketed over the past couple of years. This is because podcasting can be a relatively easy and inexpensive way to reach a wider audience. Putting out a few well-produced episodes featuring compelling content can be much more effective than purchasing traditional advertisements. For those who want to create high quality content without investing in expensive equipment, Full Stack Media can produce your podcast for you. Our professionals will take care of set up, recording, editing and uploading—you literally only have to show up and talk!
